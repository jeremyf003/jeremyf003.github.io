Problem 12

Evaluate the limit:

\[ \lim_{x \to 2} \frac{x^2 - 4}{x - 2} \]

Factor the numerator:

\[ x^2 - 4 = (x-2)(x+2) \]

Simplifying:

\[ \frac{(x-2)(x+2)}{x-2} = x+2 \]

Therefore:

\[ \lim_{x \to 2} (x+2) = 4 \]
